About Me

With a passion for helping people achieve their health goals through proper nutrition, I bring scientific expertise and personalized care to each client.

Laboni Akter

I am a graduate from the Institute of Nutrition and Food Science, University of Dhaka, with both undergraduate and ongoing postgraduate studies in nutrition science. Currently, I am working as a nutritionist at American Wellness Centre (AWC).

My goal is to enhance your life through science-based nutritional advice and bring new vitality to your health. I address every aspect of health issues—from gastric problems to complex chronic diseases.

I believe that proper nutrition is not just about treating diseases but is a powerful tool for improving quality of life. My main objective is to provide personalized, scientific, and effective nutritional solutions for each patient.

My Specializations

Comprehensive nutritional solutions for a wide range of health conditions and wellness goals.

Gastric & IBS

Proper nutrition planning to reduce stomach complications and manage Irritable Bowel Syndrome effectively.

CKD (Kidney Disease)

Special dietary habits and nutritional guidance for kidney care and management of chronic kidney disease.

Obesity & Diabetes

Weight control and blood sugar management through proper dietary planning and lifestyle changes.

Allergies & Skin Conditions

Diet plans and lifestyle advice to restore skin health for conditions like allergies, vitiligo, and psoriasis.

Weakness & Autism

The role of nutrition in improving physical and mental health, including support for autism spectrum disorders.

Heart Disease & Hypertension

Long-term pain and blood pressure control through dietary and lifestyle coordination.

Insomnia & Thyroid

The powerful role of nutrition in various body complexities including sleep disorders and thyroid conditions.

PCOS & Women's Health

Specialized nutritional plans for women's health issues including PCOS, anemia, and mental health improvement.
